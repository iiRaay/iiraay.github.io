Who is Frédéric François Chopin?

Frédéric Chopin was a child prodigy born in Poland who later then settled in France. His career as a composer and pianist of the romantic era first began when he published his first composition at the age of 7 which lead him to perform on stage one year later.

Throughout his career, Chopin hated public performances and only performed 30 public concerts. In addition to his concerts, he composed some of the greatest piano pieces of the era (a full list can be found here) which encapsulated a wide variety of genres including preludes, nocturnes, waltzes, sonatas, and etudes.
